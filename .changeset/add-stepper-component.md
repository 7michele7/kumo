feat(stepper): add `Stepper` component

New `Stepper` component for multi-step flows, with `useStepper`/`useStep` hooks,
horizontal and vertical orientations, and composable `Step`, `StepHeader`,
`StepPanel`, `StepIndicator`, `StepperFooter`, `StepperBack`, and `StepperNext`
parts. Exported from the package root and as a dedicated `components/stepper`
entry point.
